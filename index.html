Frequently Asked

Got questions?
We have answers.

How long does it take to build my website?
Most landing pages and single-page sites are delivered within 5–7 business days. Multi-page sites typically take 2–3 weeks depending on content and revisions. We move fast without cutting corners — first draft is usually in your hands within 48 hours of receiving your content.
Do I own my website once it's built?
Yes — 100%. Once your project is complete and paid, the site is entirely yours. We don't hold your files or domain hostage. You can take it anywhere you want, though most clients stick around for our monthly upkeep plans because it's just easier.
What do I need to provide to get started?
Not much — that's the point of our discovery call. Generally helpful to have: your logo (if you have one), brand colors, any existing photos, and an idea of what you like. If you don't have photos yet, that's what our brand photography packages are for. We guide you through everything.
What's included in monthly upkeep?
Monthly upkeep covers text and image updates, security and performance monitoring, seasonal design refreshes, and priority support. Think of it as having a web team on retainer — you message us, we handle it. Plans start at $59/mo depending on your package.
Can you work with businesses outside Southern California?
Absolutely. Web design, content strategy, and video editing are fully remote. Brand photography requires an in-person shoot — we're based in Southern California so local clients get the most out of that service, but we're open to travel for the right project.
How does the discovery call work?
It's a free 20–30 minute call — no pressure, no pitch. We go over your business, your goals, what you like and don't like, and which package makes the most sense for where you're at. By the end you'll have a clear picture of exactly what we'd build and what it costs. Book yours here.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. For larger projects we typically split into 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. Custom payment arrangements can be discussed on your discovery call — we want to make it work for you.
What makes SERA different from other agencies?
We don't do templates, we don't do cookie-cutter. Every site is built from scratch around your brand and your goals. We also combine web design, photography, and content creation under one roof — so everything is cohesive and on-brand from day one. You get a partner, not just a vendor.
